Brief Bio: I am an assistant professor, jointly affiliated to the Artificial Intelligence Graduate School (AIGS) and Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at UNIST since April 2020. I obtained BS (2009) and MS (2011) degrees from Dept. of Electrical Engineering at KAIST and Ph.D. degree (2020) from Dept. of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Imperial College London. I have interned at INRIA Sophia Antipolis (2019.6-2019.8.) and was a Post-doctoral Research Assistant at University of Oxford (2019.11-2020.2). Before joining Ph.D., I was an employee at DMC Research Center of Samsung Electronics for four years (2011.2.-2015.2.).

Research Interests

  • Next generation integrated energy systems
